User Need

I need to easily go back to the first step of a multi-step process.

Related User Story: Placeholder.

What to Do

Make sure starting points of processes are:

  • easy to get to,
  • easy to orient,
  • easy to find on each screen of the process.

For example: Clearly indicate the first step of a process. Make the first step more visible and identifiable. Use a predictable location, larger sizes, strong color contrast, and appealing icons to make the starting point stand out.

[Note:While making the first step easily identifiable, make sure that the current step should be the most visually prominent.]

How it Helps

Sometimes users make mistakes and need to check their work. Other times users get lost and disoriented during a process. The struggle to complete a process is often aggravated by stress, mental health and/or cognitive overload. Allowing users to return directly to the beginning means they can check their work and orient themselves or restart if they become disoriented or lost.

Making strating step more visible and recognizable provides a more efficient way for people with cognitive disabilities to reorient themselves to complete their intended tasks.
